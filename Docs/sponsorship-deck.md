## 1. The one-liner

**BDQ Social** is Vadodara's most premium, curated lifestyle festival and night market — a
Pinterest-grade weekend at Aarush Lawns, right before Diwali, where the city's highest-spending,
most aesthetic-driven crowd comes to shop, eat, and vibe. Sponsoring BDQ Social puts your brand
in front of that crowd, in a clean, premium setting they actually want to be in.

## 2. Why this audience is worth it

- **Who:** Vadodara's Gen-Z, Millennials, young families, and visiting NRIs.
- **Profile:** high disposable income, brand-conscious, heavy Instagram users, the crowd that
  says "there's nothing good to do in Baroda" — and shows up when there finally is.
- **Mindset on-site:** they came to spend — shopping (80+ curated indie brands), gourmet food,
  experiences. Buying intent is already switched on.
- **Setting:** carpeted, fairy-lit, boho, premium. Your brand sits in an aspirational environment,
  not a dusty mela.

> Exact reach/footfall numbers are filled from registration + ticketing data once live (the portal
> tracks every registration, ticket, and check-in). Use this deck's structure; drop in the live
> numbers per edition.

## 3. The event at a glance

- **Venue:** Aarush Lawn, Vadodara (~230 × 160 ft master layout).
- **Capacity footprint:** 101 stalls (36 small, 32 lane, 16 premium, 10 food), 4 activity zones,
  a 40 × 24 ft main stage, food + beverage partner lounges.
- **Format:** sundowner acoustic sets → high-energy night; shopping + food + live music +
  Instagrammable spaces.
- **Timing:** evening event (4–11 PM), pre-Diwali weekend — peak gifting + going-out season.

## 6. Where and how we market you

- **Digital:** your logo/credit on the landing site, ticket flow, and confirmation messages seen
  by every buyer; feature in pre-event WhatsApp + email campaigns; social posts/reels and
  share-reward campaigns; UTM-tagged links so reach is measurable.
- **On-ground:** entry LED wall, stage backdrop, branded zone signage, standees, sampling and
  activation space, announcements from stage.
- **On the map:** sponsored pins and an owned, labelled zone visible to everyone browsing the
  interactive floor plan.

## 7. What you get back — the data

Sponsors with data access receive a **post-event report** drawn straight from the portal:

- **Footfall:** total check-ins, peak hours, live capacity curve, in/out flow.
- **Audience:** registration + ticket demographics (age band, source/UTM channel, repeat vs new),
  geo/pin-code mix (where buyers came from) — aggregated, privacy-respecting.
- **Engagement at your zone/stall:** scan counts, lead-capture contacts (with consent), footfall
  heatmap of busiest zones.
- **Reach:** impressions across site, tickets, and WhatsApp/email comms; social-share campaign
  performance.
- **Format:** one-click PDF/CSV export per edition.
