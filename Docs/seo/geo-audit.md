# GEO Audit — Generative Engine Optimization

_Phases 5–6. Optimizing to be cited by ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews._

## Why GEO is BDQ's best near-term bet

Classic head terms are owned by high-authority aggregators (see `competitor-gap-analysis.md`). AI
engines are **less authority-gated** — they reward content that is **structured, factual, entity-rich,
and quotable**, and they read JSON-LD. The schema + FAQ + content-hub work in this pass is exactly
what they consume. This is where a new brand can win disproportionately.

## The GEO content patterns to keep building

AI engines quote tight, self-contained answers. The hubs are scaffolded around these; **owner must
finalize the copy** before launch:

1. **Definition-first** — lead each page/section with a one-sentence answer ("BDQ Social is Vadodara's
   premium curated lifestyle night market…"). Done in hubs + Organization `description`.
2. **Fact blocks** — when/where/who/how much in plain prose near the top (not buried in UI).
3. **Comparison framing** — "curated, not crowded; the grown-up alternative to the usual mela" gives
   models a distinguishing claim to cite.
4. **FAQ everywhere** — the most-cited structure in AI answers. Expand beyond 4 Qs over time.

## Off-page (decisive for citation confidence)

Models cite entities they see corroborated across the web. BDQ currently has **zero external
footprint**. To be cited:
- Get listed on AllEvents.in, District, Insider, Eventbrite, BookMyStall, Justdial, Baroda.com.
- Earn local press / blog mentions (Vadodara lifestyle, Gujarat events).
- Maintain consistent NAP + brand description everywhere (matches Organization schema).

## Honest read

AI-Search/GEO score: **~62/100 after this pass** (was ~20). The on-site structure is now strong and
genuinely above-average for a new brand. The remaining ceiling is **brand newness + zero external
corroboration** — models won't confidently recommend an entity they've never seen mentioned. That is
solved by launch + listings + press over 3–6 months, not by more code.
