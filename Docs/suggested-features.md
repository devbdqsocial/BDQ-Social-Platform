## Growth & Marketing
- **Referral program** (P1) — unique referral link per user; reward both sides → cheap viral growth.
- **Affiliate / promoter codes** (P2) — influencers/college reps get tracked codes + commission.
- **Influencer / press passes** (P2) — comp passes with a separate approval flow.
- **Abandoned-cart recovery** (P1) — nudge (WhatsApp/email) when checkout is started but not paid.
- **Remarketing pixels** (P2) — Meta/Google pixels for retargeting ad audiences.
- **Drip campaigns** (P2) — scheduled WhatsApp/email sequences pre-event.
- **Media / press kit page** (P2) — logos, fact sheet, photos for press and partners.
- **Blog / editorial** (P3) — SEO content ("things to do in Baroda") feeding the landing page.

## Customer Experience
- **Apple / Google Wallet passes** (P2) — ticket QR in phone wallet → premium, no-app feel.
- **Favorite / wishlist stalls** (P2) — save brands to visit → ties into navigation.
- **In-event navigation** (P3) — "you are here" + route to a stall on the 2D map.
- **Push notifications** (P1) — event-day reminder, gate-open, "your fav band is on" (PWA push).
- **SMS fallback** (P1) — if WhatsApp ticket delivery fails, fall back to SMS.
- **Multi-currency display** (P3) — for NRIs browsing prices.
- **Saved attendee details** (P3) — faster repeat checkout for returning users.
- **Exit feedback / NPS survey** (P2) — post-event survey link on the ticket → quality signal.
- **Wheelchair / accessible route** (P3) — accessibility layer on the map.
- **Weather contingency alerts** (P2) — push/WhatsApp if timings shift due to weather.

## In-event Tech
- **Cashless wallet / RFID wristbands** (P3) — top-up + tap-to-pay at stalls; big ops lift.
- **Live "stalls left" / sold-out counter** (P1) — scarcity on the landing page.
- **Countdown timer to event** (P1) — urgency on the landing page.
- **Map mini-game / treasure hunt** (P3) — scan stalls to complete a trail → reward.
- **Live announcements feed** (P2) — push "set starting", "flash sale at F-12" to attendees.

## Vendor
- **Stall footfall analytics** (P2) — scan-at-stall counts → vendors see traffic to their stall.
- **Vendor add-ons ordering** (P2) — order extra tables, chairs, power, signage during setup.
- **Insurance add-on** (P3) — optional stall/goods insurance at checkout.
- **Co-vendor / stall sharing** (P3) — two small brands share one stall with split billing.
- **Vendor performance leaderboard** (P3) — gamify scans/ratings across vendors.
- **Vendor onboarding wizard + checklist** (P2) — guided steps to a complete, approvable profile.
- **Vendor ↔ organizer chat** (P2) — in-portal support thread instead of scattered WhatsApp.

## Admin & Operations
- **Dedicated multi-gate scanner mode** (P1) — kiosk UI, gate selection, big scan target.
- **Dynamic / surge pricing** (P3) — auto-raise ticket price as inventory drops.
- **Incident / lost-and-found log** (P3) — on-ground ops tracking.
- **Volunteer / crew management** (P3) — shifts, check-in, task assignment for event staff.
- **Bulk import/export** (P2) — CSV import of vendors/coupons, export of registrations.
- **Config-driven landing CMS** (P3) — edit hero/copy/sections without a deploy.

## Engagement & On-ground
- **Gamified stall "passport"** (P2) — collect stamps/scans across stalls → unlock a reward.
- **Spin-the-wheel / scratch card** (P3) — at entry or checkout → discounts, freebies.
- **Contests & live polls** (P3) — drive stage interaction and social posting.
- **Photo-booth gallery upload** (P3) — attendees upload booth photos to a branded gallery.

## Monetization & Finance
- **Cross-event loyalty / membership** (P3) — points across editions → returning discounts.
- **Gift cards** (P3) — buy credit, redeem against tickets.
- **Corporate / B2B bulk ticket sales** (P2) — invoiced bulk orders for companies/colleges.
- **Split / installment vendor payments** (P3) — pay stall fee in parts.
- **Settlement dashboard** (P2) — reconcile Razorpay payouts vs bookings.
- **Sponsor self-serve portal** (P3) — sponsors see their placements + live lead/scan data.

## Tech & Scale
- **Real-time map via websockets** (P2) — instant stall status updates (vs polling in MVP).
- **Multi-language** (P2) — Gujarati / Hindi / English for Vadodara's mixed audience.
- **CRM integration** (P3) — sync attendees/leads to a CRM.
- **FAQ chatbot / live chat widget** (P3) — deflect support questions on the landing page.
- **Testimonials / social proof** (P3) — past-edition photos + quotes on the landing page.
- **Re-entry stamp / wristband** (P2) — allow leave-and-return without re-scanning a ticket.

## Data & Insights
- **A/B testing on landing / pricing** (P3) — optimize conversion.
- **Geo / pin-code heatmap of buyers** (P3) — where attendees come from → ad targeting.
- **Vendor mix optimization** (P3) — which categories drive footfall/sales → curate next edition.
